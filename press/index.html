Company overview

HelpMeWrite is an AI-powered email writing platform built specifically for professionals across 12 industries. Unlike generic AI tools, HelpMeWrite uses profession-specific AI system prompts — trained on the language, tone, and communication conventions of each field — to generate polished, ready-to-send emails in under 10 seconds.

The platform is powered by Claude, Anthropic's AI model, and supports real estate agents, lawyers, sales representatives, HR recruiters, freelancers, doctors, consultants, financial advisors, property managers, coaches, accountants, and personal trainers.
