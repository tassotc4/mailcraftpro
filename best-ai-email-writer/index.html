The problem with generic AI email tools

ChatGPT, Claude, and other general-purpose AI tools can write emails — but they write generic emails. They don't know that a real estate agent's follow-up after a showing should feel warm and low-pressure. They don't know that a lawyer's client update should be precise and measured. They don't know that a sales rep's break-up email should feel final but leave the door open.

Profession-specific context matters enormously in email — and that's what most AI tools miss.
