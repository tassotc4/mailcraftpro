Frequently asked questions about pricing

How much does HelpMeWrite cost?

HelpMeWrite offers four pricing tiers: Free (3 emails, no credit card), Starter at $9/month for 100 emails, Pro at $19/month for unlimited emails plus Brand Kit, and Pro Annual at $149/year — saving 35% versus monthly billing.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — every new account gets 3 free email generations with no credit card required. You can also earn more free emails by referring friends. Each referral earns you both 5 bonus emails with no cap.

What is the Brand Kit?

Brand Kit is a Pro feature that lets you save your name, company, role, and preferred tone. Every email you generate is automatically personalized with your details — no retyping required.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing period.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings with no fees or penalties. You retain access to your plan until the end of your current billing period.

Do unused emails roll over?

On the Starter plan, unused emails do not roll over to the next month. On the Pro plan, emails are unlimited so rollover isn't applicable.
