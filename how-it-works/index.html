How it works — 3 steps

1

Select your profession

Choose from 12 professions. This tells HelpMeWrite which AI system prompt to use — each one is tuned to the language, tone, and communication norms of that specific industry.

Example
You select "Real Estate Agent" → HelpMeWrite knows you write to buyers, sellers, and other agents, and that your emails should be warm, professional, and action-oriented.
2

Describe your email

Tell HelpMeWrite what you need in plain English — one or two sentences is enough. No templates to fill in, no fields to complete. Just describe the situation naturally, like you'd explain it to a colleague.

Example inputs
"Follow-up to buyer James who saw 42 Oak Street yesterday. He seemed interested in the garden but worried about the price."
"Rejection email to candidate Sarah for the Marketing Manager role. She made it to final round — she was great, just not quite the right fit."
"Invoice follow-up for client who is 2 weeks late on a $3,500 payment. This is the second reminder."
3

Get your email — in under 10 seconds

HelpMeWrite generates a complete, ready-to-send email instantly. Review it, copy it to your email client, and send. Not quite right? Adjust the tone or regenerate — no limits.

What you get
A complete email with subject line, greeting, body, and sign-off — tuned to your profession and situation. Formatted and ready to copy directly into Gmail, Outlook, or any email client.

Frequently asked questions

How does HelpMeWrite generate emails?

HelpMeWrite uses Claude AI by Anthropic with profession-specific system prompts. Select your profession, describe what you need, and the AI generates a complete email tuned to your industry's communication conventions in under 10 seconds.

How long does it take?

Most emails are generated in under 10 seconds. The entire workflow from opening the app to copying a finished email typically takes under 60 seconds.

Can I edit the generated email?

Yes. Every generated email is fully editable. Copy it into your email client and adjust any details. You can also regenerate with different context or change the tone.

Does HelpMeWrite send emails for me?

No — HelpMeWrite generates the copy for you to copy and send from your own email client (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.). You stay in full control of your communications.

What AI model powers HelpMeWrite?

HelpMeWrite is powered by Claude, made by Anthropic — chosen for its ability to produce nuanced, natural professional writing that doesn't sound like generic AI output.
