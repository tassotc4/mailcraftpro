HelpMeWrite was born from a simple frustration — watching skilled professionals spend 20 minutes writing an email that should take 2. We built the tool we wished existed.

The problem we're solving

Professionals across every industry spend a disproportionate amount of their day writing emails. A real estate agent drafting follow-ups after showings. A recruiter writing rejection letters. A freelancer crafting cold outreach. A consultant updating clients on project status.

These emails are important — they shape client relationships, drive deals forward, and represent your professional brand. But they're also routine. The same types of emails, written over and over, often from scratch.

Generic AI tools like ChatGPT can help, but they produce generic output. They don't know that a lawyer's email to opposing counsel should be measured and precise. They don't know that a sales rep's break-up email should feel final but leave the door open. They don't know that a medical follow-up needs to be both clear and reassuring.

Profession-specific context matters enormously in email — and that's the gap HelpMeWrite fills.
